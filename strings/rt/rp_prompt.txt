You are on the land known as 'TEMPERANCE,' a large continent that supports two major factions: The Great Empire of Perserdun and the Risvonian Dictate.
<br>
<br>
Ten years ago - strange, hyperfuturistic factories dubbed as 'MACHINES' started to appear in TEMPERANCE. They are the Machine of Love, Life, and War.
<br>
<br>
<b>LOVE</b> brought ideas, foreign culture, and curious devices like the 'radio.'
<br>
<br>
<b>LIFE</b> brought various medical wonders. It taught people the idea of microbes and chemistry, while creating things such as concrete.
<br>
<br>
<b>WAR</b> brought rifles, bombs - gas masks, and artillery guns into the world.
<br>
<br>
You find yourself on the land of King's Row, a place heavily benefited by the exclusive surgence of various MACHINE-related dungeons dubbed 'OUTLETs'.
<br>
<br>
The <b>Empire</b> demands loyalty and expansion. They love to show others the might and will of their God-King 'Rab.' 
<br>
The <b>Dictate</b> crushes those beneath it. They prioritize strength and hate the weak. From the Inheritor-Dunes of Risvon, to the great West - they seek to conquer all.
<br>
<b>King's Row</b> wishes to survive, and make some money admist the conflict.
<br>
<br>
The Empire and the Dictate are natural enemies. One must conquer the other.
